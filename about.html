I'm a 20 year old level 200 student of the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, Kumasi. Technology is a passion for me and I very much love it.
I'm a graphic designer, web developer, a pythonista and an AI/ML enthusiast. Refer to Projects for more.
I look forward to harnessing the power of Technology to the best benefit of man in all aspects of life.
